# **App Name**: FlexNest

## Core Features:

- AI Workspace Advisor: An intelligent recommendation engine that uses a reasoning tool to analyze team calendars and suggest the optimal WFH or in-office days for maximum productivity and sustainability.
- Live Resource Floorplan: Interactive SVG map providing a real-time, color-coded view of desks, EV chargers, and meeting rooms with 60-second state updates.
- 30-Second Booking Wizard: A streamlined three-step mobile flow for reserving any office asset up to 30 days in advance.
- Eco-Incentive Dashboard: Gamified tracker calculating CO2 savings and commute time reduced, converting them into redeemable points and visual badges.
- Team Presence Planner: A collaborative calendar showing team locations to prevent overcrowding and ensure intentional face-to-face collaboration days.
- Atomic Conflict Resolver: A high-performance booking backend implementing transaction locks and 15-minute auto-release timers for unoccupied resources.
- SaaS Admin Console: A reporting hub for heatmaps, occupancy trends, and policy enforcement to manage organizational capacity and costs.

## Style Guidelines:

- The palette is inspired by sustainability and professional clarity. We use a deep dark scheme to reduce eye strain for hybrid workers.
- Primary color: Mint Sage (#66CCB3) chosen for its calming and eco-conscious vibe. Background color: Deep Charcoal (#161D1B), a desaturated variant of the primary hue for a sophisticated dark mode.
- Accent color: Vibrant Verdant (#99F2AF), providing high contrast against the dark background for critical status indicators and action buttons.
- A font pairing strategy: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for tech-forward headlines and 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clean, highly-legible body text across various devices.
- Minimalist line icons for resources like desks and EV chargers, utilizing the 'color-coded availability' system for instant visual status recognition.
- A mobile-first, card-based interface using progressive disclosure to keep information density low while providing easy access to detailed bookings.
- Subtle micro-interactions on the floorplan and rewards dashboard, with soft fade transitions between scheduling states.
